# Evidence and Attribution — Brunson Method

## Source theory

**Russell Brunson** is the founder of **ClickFunnels** and author of:
- **DotCom Secrets** (2015) — funnel architecture
- **Expert Secrets** (2017) — Origin Story, mass movement building, perfect webinar
- **Traffic Secrets** (2020) — channel strategy

The frameworks documented in this genome are distilled from these books and validated across thousands of funnels published on the ClickFunnels platform.

## Brunson the man (biographical context)

- **Background:** failed in network marketing for 5 years before ClickFunnels success
- **ClickFunnels founding:** 2014, with Todd Dickerson
- **Mentor lineage:** Dan Kennedy (direct response), Tony Robbins (mass-market influence), various coaches across his career
- **Brand:** "the king of funnels" / "the biggest funnel hacker"
- **ClickFunnels scale:** over 200k+ users at peak, billions in tracked revenue across user funnels

His method is **operationally validated** across the ClickFunnels platform — every framework in this genome has been deployed in tens of thousands of real funnels with measurable conversion results.

The Brunson lineage **dominates the modern info-product / course launch space**. Most course launches in 2026 use SOME version of his method.

These patterns appear across his communication — books, webinars, podcasts. They're functional (story-anchoring openers) and brand-distinctive.
